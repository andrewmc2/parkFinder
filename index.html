    Welcome to Toronto Park Finder!

    Let me help you find a great park or community centre near you!

    On the top menu, you can filter the parks you want to visit by type. Currently available are dog zones, playground, pools, rinks and fire-pits. The section right underneath the map will show you the closest parks. Click on the map markers to find the best parks near you and then click on the link name to reveal Instagram pics of the park (or a cat rejecting you). If you like what you see, click the directions link and you will be given step by step instructions to get to the park!
